About Us

Experience the cozy atmosphere of Caffe Italia, a popular spot in Saranda. Enjoy a variety of drinks, from cocktails to soft drinks and beer. Our menu caters to all tastes, whether you want a snack or a full meal. Come to Caffe Italia for a friendly vibe and great memories with friends in Saranda.

Our Facilities



Cozy Atmosphere

Caffe Italia offers a cozy and inviting atmosphere, ideal for starting your day off right with a warm cup of coffee and a delicious breakfast treat.

Exquisite Coffee Selection

Dive into a world of flavor with our extensive selection of premium coffees, expertly brewed to perfection for your enjoyment.

Gelato

Treat yourself to the creamy goodness of gelato, available in a variety of tantalizing flavors that will transport your taste buds to heaven.

Signature Cocktails

Elevate your evening with our signature cocktails, expertly crafted using only the finest spirits and freshest ingredients for a taste sensation like no other.

Refreshing Beverages

Quench your thirst with a variety of refreshing beverages, including freshly squeezed juices etc perfect for accompanying your morning meal.

Friendly Service

Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing friendly and efficient service, ensuring that your dining experience is nothing short of exceptional.

Outdoor Seating Area

Enjoy the fresh air and scenic views from our outdoor seating area, the perfect spot to sip your coffee and savor your food in the morning sun.

Relaxed Environment

Take a moment to unwind and enjoy the relaxed ambiance, where you can take your time to enjoy your meal and start your day off at your own pace.
